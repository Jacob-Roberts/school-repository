Orangutan is not spelled with a G at the end, contrary to popular belief

Orangutans arms are so long, they can scratch their ankles without bending over.

Orangutans are mammals

The word Orangutan comes from the malay word for "human of the forest"

Even though they could be carnivores, and become the real king of the forest, they choose to eat only fruits and leaves
